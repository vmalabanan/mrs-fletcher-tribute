Fletcher

Murder, She Wrote protagonist,

mystery writer,

and sleuth

From a Los Angeles Times obit

on Dame Angela Lansbury,

who played J.B. Fletcher:

Smart, sensible, a bit sassy, very literate and occasionally open to romance, Jessica Fletcher was not rumpled, haunted or conflicted. She was a woman you might know or even be, if only you had her confidence. . . . Jessica Fletcher was something that is still rare on television: a woman who understands her own worth. . . . She was a force in any room, and solved her mysteries through the simple process of critical thinking and paying attention.

Mary McNamara

"Angela Lansbury in ‘Murder, She Wrote’ wasn’t ‘cozy’ — she was revolutionary"
